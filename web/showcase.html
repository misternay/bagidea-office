Show your work

Get featured here

Made something with BagIdea Office? Send it in — it's a great way to show your work, and you're credited as a contributor.

1 · Grab a cover

Take a wide (~16:9) screenshot or image of your work and add it to web/img/showcase/ in the repo.

2 · Add your entry

Append an entry to web/showcase.json — title, your GitHub handle, the image path, a link, and a short description.

3 · Open a PR

We review and merge it preserving your authorship, so you land on the Contributors graph and your card appears here.
