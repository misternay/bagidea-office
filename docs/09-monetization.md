# 9. Monetization Possibilities

## 9.1 Principles

1. **Never tax the agents** — users already pay for LLM tokens; charging per-task or per-agent would punish exactly the behavior we want. Monetize the *experience and the leverage*, not the throughput.
2. **Free tier must be genuinely lovable** — the wallpaper itself is the viral artifact; crippling it kills the growth loop (screenshots/streams are the marketing).
3. **B2C funds the romance, B2B funds the company.**

## 9.2 Tier Structure

| | **Free — "Startup"** | **Pro — "Scale-up"** ($8–12/mo or $99 lifetime-ish founders) | **Team — "Company"** ($15/seat/mo) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Agents rendered | up to 5 | unlimited | unlimited |
| Zones & features | all functional | all | all |
| Adapters | Claude Code, MCP, generic | + all premium adapters, adapter priority support | + SSO, admin |
| Themes | default | premium themes, seasonal packs included | + custom branding (your logo in the lobby) |
| Replay Theater | last 24h | full history | full + export/audit |
| Voice | push-to-talk, local TTS | wake word, premium voices | + meeting transcription |
| Multi-monitor / tower mode | 1 monitor | multi-monitor, 100+ scale | shared office, team board |
| Mobile companion | view-only | full control | full + team alerts |
| Workflow Designer | 3 saved flows | unlimited | shared flow library |

The free 5-agent render cap is the cleanest functional-feeling-but-fair lever: the *daemon* still runs unlimited agents (no productivity tax — law #1); beyond 5, extra agents appear as silhouettes in the office ("contractors") — visible, working, but uncharacterized. Upgrading "gives your whole team faces." Emotionally compelling, functionally harmless.

## 9.3 Cosmetic Commerce

- **Theme packs** ($3–6): cyberpunk tower, woodland studio, retro 90s, spaceship bridge, seasonal sets.
- **Character packs** ($2–4): sprite outfit sets, hairstyles, accessories, desk pets.
- **Office props**: arcade cabinet, aquarium, neon signs.
- Earned soft currency (Coffee Beans) buys a rotating subset of these for free — keeps the store from feeling predatory and gives progression a sink.

## 9.4 Marketplace (platform revenue)

- **Agent templates** (persona+prompt+toolset+sprite) and **workflow blueprints**, community-priced $0–15, platform takes 20–30%.
- **Adapter bounties**: fund community adapters for long-tail agent frameworks.
- Curated "Verified" program (security-reviewed, sandbox-tested) — trust is the product here.

## 9.5 B2B / Enterprise

- **Team office** (shared world, real shared task board) is the wedge — it turns a desktop toy into the visible heartbeat of a team's automation. Pricing per seat.
- **Enterprise** (custom): on-prem daemon fleet, audit-grade replay/journal export, policy controls (centrally managed Security Center), SLA. The Security/Replay architecture is already audit-shaped — sell it.
- **Conference-room mode**: the office on the wall TV of a real office — a literal live dashboard of the company's AI workforce. (Status-symbol sales channel; demos itself.)

## 9.6 Non-Obvious Channels

- **OEM/partner**: agent-framework vendors (or model providers) pay for first-class launch adapters / co-marketing ("works beautifully with X").
- **Creator program**: streamers showing their office = free marketing; affiliate codes on Pro.
- **Founders' lifetime edition** at launch: funds runway, builds an evangelist core, includes an exclusive lobby statue (vanity that costs nothing).

## 9.7 What We Will Not Do

No ads in the world. No selling telemetry/usage data. No paywalled safety features (Security Center is free, forever — charging for safety is disqualifying). No energy/stamina mechanics on real work.

## 9.8 Sequencing

1. **Launch**: Free + Pro + founders lifetime + 2 theme packs (validate willingness-to-pay on beauty).
2. **+6 mo**: marketplace beta (templates/workflows), seasonal packs cadence.
3. **+12 mo**: Team tier (the retention/expansion engine), conference-room mode.
4. **+18 mo**: Enterprise once replay/audit/policy hardening is proven with teams.
